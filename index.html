In today's world, it feels like every day we hear about another crime that takes place, violent or non-violent, though the violent ones are the ones that tend to be on our radars more. I wanted to know if crimes were happening more often today or we're just hearing about them more often than we used to. According to FBI's crime data page, crime rates in general actually decreased in the last 10 years. So statistically, we're just hearing about crime more than we used to. A possible explanation is that media is able to spread the word much easier now than in 2007. Twitter and Facebook allow for news to be shared much sooner, almost immediately after something happens.
